# Heart-Disease-Data-Analysis-Dashboard
This report presents a comprehensive analysis of a heart disease dataset using Microsoft Power BI. The project covers:
•	Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
•	Risk segmentation based on clinical indicators
•	Analytical modeling using DAX
•	Development of an interactive cardiovascular risk dashboard
The objective of this analysis is to:
•	Identify demographic and clinical patterns associated with heart disease
•	Evaluate behavioral and metabolic risk factors
•	Measure overall disease prevalence
•	Develop a decision-support dashboard for healthcare insight
From the analysis:
•	Total Patients: 10,000
•	Total Heart Disease Cases: 2,000
•	Disease Rate: 20%
•	Average Age: 49.2 years
•	High-Risk Patients: 31.8%
The findings indicate that smoking, diabetes severity, cholesterol level, BMI category, and inflammatory markers are strong contributors to heart disease prevalence.
Key Analytical Findings
•	Heart disease prevalence decreased significantly with age
•	Smoking is one of the strongest modifiable risk factors
•	High diabetes severity greatly increases heart disease likelihood
•	Obesity category dominate heart disease cases
•	High CRP indicates inflammation-driven cardiovascular risk
•	Cholesterol and triglyceride elevation reinforce metabolic risk
•	High Stage 2 & High Stage 1 of Blood Pressure dominates heart disease cases
This project successfully transformed multi-factor cardiovascular data into a structured, interactive risk intelligence dashboard.
The analysis demonstrates:
•	The power of demographic segmentation
•	The strong predictive influence of smoking and diabetes
•	The compounding effect of metabolic and inflammatory indicators
